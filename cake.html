🍫 Chocolate Cake Recipe

Prep Time
20 mins
Cook Time
35 mins
Servings
8

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Grease and flour a cake pan.
  3. Mix flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  4. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract.
  5. Beat until the batter is smooth.
  6. Pour into the prepared cake pan.
  7. Bake for 35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Let the cake cool before frosting and serving.

Tips

  • Use room-temperature eggs for better texture.
  • Add chocolate chips for extra richness.
  • Decorate with whipped cream and berries.
